Welcome Aboard

The Bella Luna is a stunning 2024 Bali 4.2 Catamaran, designed for those who seek the perfect balance of performance and comfort. Featuring the signature "Open Space" design, the Bella Luna seamlessly connects the aft cockpit and saloon with a tilting garage door, creating a massive living area for you and your guests.

Whether you are relaxing on the solid foredeck, enjoying a sunset from the flybridge, or resting in the luxurious owner's suite, the Bella Luna offers a sailing experience unlike any other.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your charter

What sailing qualifications do I need?

To charter the Bella Luna as a bareboat, the skipper must have relevant sailing experience on vessels of similar size. A formal certification (like ASA or RYA) is preferred but a sailing resume demonstrating competence may be accepted. If you do not meet the requirements, we can provide a licensed captain for your trip.

What is included on the yacht?

The yacht comes fully equipped with all navigation electronics, safety gear, a fully stocked galley (kitchenware), linens, and towels (bath and beach). A RIB dinghy with an outboard motor is also included in your charter fee.

Can we hire a captain or chef?

Absolutely. If you prefer a more relaxed vacation, we can arrange for a professional captain and/or a chef. Please note that the crew will require their own cabin and meals must be provided for them.

What time does the charter start and end?

Standard charters begin at 12:00 PM (noon) on the first day and end at 12:00 PM on the final day. Sleep-aboard options for the night prior to your start date may be available upon request.

Is there WiFi on board?

Yes, the Bella Luna is equipped with a high-speed WiFi router. Data packages can be purchased at the base before departure so you can stay connected while exploring the islands.
